üìã ABSTRACT

This project presents an Ethical AI Advisor, a knowledge-based expert system implemented in Prolog that performs automated moral reasoning. The system analyzes ethical dilemmas using four established philosophical frameworks: Utilitarianism, Deontology, Virtue Ethics, and Care Ethics. By formalizing ethical principles as logical rules and facts, the system provides justified recommendations with complete reasoning traces, demonstrating how artificial intelligence can engage with complex moral questions.
